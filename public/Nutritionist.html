Nutritionist

Laura King

Dietitian & Exercise Physiologist

Laura is experienced in assisting with lifestyle change as a dual qualified Dietitian and Exercise Physiologist. She is keen to help you establish a calm, confident food relationship; and is passionate about movement for mind wellness. Her health-focused care has an emphasis on moderation and balance.

Reene Harudika

Dietitian

Renee has been practising clinically in Brisbane and Gold Coast for over 6 years. She is passionate about helping you to establish a healthy relationship with food and making lasting change to your life. Her focus is on guiding you to find balance and to get enjoyment from food without guilt.

Amanda Clark

Principal Dietitian

Amanda Clark is the principal dietitian at Great Ideas in Nutrition and has been practising since 1987. She is known for her practical ideas and concepts which culminated in the creation of the bestselling Portion Perfection – a visual weight control plan in 2008. Amanda is the recipient of two national awards for her ideas.

Gillian Woodward

Dietitian

Practising since 1981, Gillian's experience includes both community and clinical dietetics for 16 years in Armidale.Gillian carries out home visit services for frail and unwell elderly in the area from Murwillumbah to Robina. Gillian’s skilled and caring services assist individuals to maintain weight.
